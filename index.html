Vision & Impact

Transforming Starlab into an orbital workshop.

Near-Term Impact

  • Accelerates material science in microgravity
  • Validates dust-mitigation and containment technologies
  • Reduces operational costs through autonomy and recycling

Long-Term Impact

  • Demonstrates essential capabilities for orbital industry
  • Enables planetary ISRU pathways for Moon and Mars
  • Supports sustainable deep-space exploration

Circular Economy in Space

  • Closed-loop material reuse and in-orbit recycling
  • Contributes to the transition from Zero Debris to a circular economy
  • Repositions Starlab as a hub for sustainable orbital industry

From Research Outpost to Orbital Workshop
